My apps list

  • Maccy: Clipboard manager for macOS. Open source.
  • warp: Warp is the terminal with AI and your dev team’s knowledge built-in. A rust terminal.
  • karabiner-elements: A powerful and stable keyboard customizer for macOS.
  • MonitorControl: Controls your external display brightness and volume and shows native OSD. Open source.
  • ChatX: AI local agent.
  • Stats: macOS system monitor in your menu bar. Open source.
  • Barbee: Manage my menu bar. ¥22/year or $28 forever.
  • Microsoft Remote Desktop beta: As it’s name. Beta clients should not be used in a production environment.ß
